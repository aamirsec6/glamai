# Pricing & Packaging

## Plans

### Starter — ₹1,999/mo (~$24/mo)

**For:** Solo interior designers just starting out

**Includes:**
- GBP Optimizer (4 posts/month)
- WhatsApp AI Agent (lead qualification)
- Monthly value report
- Lead dashboard
- Email support (48h response)

**Guarantee:** Top 3 rank target, no exclusivity

**Limits:**
- 1 WhatsApp number
- 100 leads/month
- 5 keyword tracking

---

### Growth — ₹4,999/mo (~$60/mo)

**For:** Established designers who want to scale

**Everything in Starter, plus:**
- 8 GBP posts/month
- Competitor monitoring (5 competitors)
- Bi-weekly ranking reports
- Priority keyword optimization
- WhatsApp + Email support (24h response)

**Guarantee:** Top 3 rank target, no exclusivity, priority optimization

**Limits:**
- 2 WhatsApp numbers
- 500 leads/month
- 15 keyword tracking

---

### Enterprise — ₹7,999/mo (~$96/mo)

**For:** Multi-location firms or those who want market dominance

**Everything in Growth, plus:**
- 12 GBP posts/month
- Territory exclusivity (5km radius)
- Dedicated keyword territory (6+ keywords)
- Monthly strategy call
- Priority support (4h response)
- Custom reporting

**Guarantee:** Top 1 rank target, exclusivity in territory

**Limits:**
- 5 WhatsApp numbers
- Unlimited leads
- 30 keyword tracking

To get the first 20 clients:
- 50% off for the first 3 months
- Starter: ₹999/mo (first 3 months)
- Growth: ₹2,499/mo (first 3 months)
- Enterprise: ₹3,999/mo (first 3 months)

**Why:** You need testimonials, case studies, and proof that it works.
Discounts are an investment in social proof.
